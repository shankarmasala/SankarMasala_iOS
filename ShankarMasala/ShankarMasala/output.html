ABOUT US


With the philosophy of ‘reaching our customer's heart through their taste buds’, we at Shankar have been successful in taking the Indian spices and taste to millions of homes across the globe. We take pride in the fact that Shankar has established India’s heritage across the world through Indian food, the Indian way.



The brand Shankar has been a pioneer in many food related offerings ranging from basic masala’s to instant mixes.


In 2016 Shankar Masala took another significant step in diversifying its portfolio by entering the ‘snack foods’ segment. Shankar has launched a mouth watering range of snacks food like wafers, namkeens and fryums in a range of flavours.
